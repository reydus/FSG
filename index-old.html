March '19 - Coast to Coast - our charity event for the Northwest Air Ambulance.

Experience several legs of a full-scaled simulated journey from the east coast of the United States all the way to the west coast. The journey will be comprised of over 5,000 simulated miles divided in 42 legs with different pilots each one, streamed straight and non-stop in Twitch
